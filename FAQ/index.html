Frequently Asked Questions

Fast answers to the most common questions about shipping, pricing, hiring, and operations.

Shipping & Booking
How do I book a shipment?▾
Three ways: 1) Instant quote on our homepage — no signup required. 2) Client portal for recurring shippers with multi-stop, scheduling, and invoice management. 3) Call us at (818) 624-0454 for dedicated account setup.
What types of freight do you haul?▾
All freight types: Container drayage (TWIC certified), LTL multi-stop, FTL dedicated lanes, refrigerated reefer (-20°F to +70°F), flatbed oversized, hazmat (H endorsement), express same-day. See our Services page for details on each.
What areas do you serve?▾
Primary: Southern California. Regional: Nevada, Arizona, Oregon, Washington. Nationwide: Coverage via our carrier partner network. Port drayage: Specifically from Port of Los Angeles and Long Beach with TWIC-certified drivers.
Do you handle hazmat shipments?▾
Yes. Our drivers have H endorsement for most hazmat classes. You must declare hazmat in advance, provide compliant packaging, and include proper placarding and documentation. We reserve the right to refuse non-compliant loads.
Can I cancel a booked shipment?▾
Free cancellation up to 4 hours before scheduled pickup. Within 4 hours: 25% dispatch fee. After driver arrives at pickup: full rate applies. We'll reschedule instead of cancel when possible.
Pricing & Payment
How much does shipping cost?▾
Base rate: $1.45/mile. Weight surcharge: $35 (10K-20K lbs), $75 (over 20K lbs). Multi-stop: $25 per additional stop. Rush: +30%. See Pricing page for our interactive calculator.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
Credit card (instant processing), ACH/wire transfer, and Net 30 terms for approved accounts with established credit. We never store card details — payments are tokenized via Stripe.
Do you offer volume discounts?▾
Yes. 10% discount on 10+ loads per month. 15% discount on 50+ loads per month. Custom pricing for dedicated-lane agreements. Contact us to set up an enterprise account.
Are there hidden fees?▾
No. Our quote includes everything. First 2 hours of loading/unloading included. After that, $50/hour detention. No fuel surcharge, no broker markup, no lumper, no accessorial fees unless you specifically request them.
Why are you cheaper than brokers?▾
Because we own our trucks and employ our drivers. Brokers add 15-30% markup on top of carrier rates. We cut the middleman out — same quality service, 15%+ less cost. See our comparison table.
Tracking & Delivery
Can I track my shipment in real-time?▾
Yes. Visit our Track page and enter your tracking number (e.g., SHP-0041). You'll see pickup status, in-transit GPS location, estimated arrival, and delivery confirmation. No login required.
What is your on-time delivery rate?▾
97.3% on-time across all shipments. Industry benchmark is 95%. We attribute this to owning our trucks, employing W-2 drivers, and real-time dispatch optimization.
What happens if my shipment is delayed?▾
You'll receive proactive notifications. Dispatch reroutes around traffic/weather automatically. For significant delays, our operations team contacts you directly. We credit delays over 2 hours against your invoice.
What if my cargo is damaged?▾
File a claim within 9 months of delivery per 49 CFR 370. Required: BOL, photos of damage, itemized cost of loss. Standard cargo insurance covers up to $100K per shipment. Higher coverage available on request. See Terms for details.
Driver Hiring
What are the requirements to drive for Wolfs?▾
Valid CDL-A or CDL-B, minimum 2 years verifiable experience in the last 5 years, clean MVR (max 2 violations in 3 years, no DUI/DWI), current DOT medical card, ability to pass drug screen, and US work authorization. See Careers for more.
How long is the hiring process?▾
5 business days on average: 15-minute AI interview → admin review (24 hrs) → background/MVR check → paid 1-day orientation → ride-along training → first solo dispatch.
What is the sign-on bonus?▾
$3,000 sign-on bonus for CDL-A and CDL-B drivers, paid in 3 installments: $1,000 at 30 days, $1,000 at 90 days, $1,000 at 6 months of employment.
What is the AI interview like?▾
A 15-minute conversation covering CDL credentials, experience, safety record, HOS knowledge, medical status, pre-trip inspection, and emergency response. You can type or use voice. No pressure — most candidates finish in under 15 min. Start your interview.
Do you hire from warehouse to driver?▾
Yes. We actively promote from within. Warehouse associates can enter our CDL training program once they've been with us 6 months. We pay for CDL school and hire you as a driver upon completion.
