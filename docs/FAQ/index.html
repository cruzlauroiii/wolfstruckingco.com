Frequently askedquestions

Quick answers about pricing, hiring, service area, and how the platform works.

How much does it cost to ship with Wolfs Trucking?

Our base rate is $1.45/mile with transparent pricing. Weight surcharges apply over 10,000 lbs. Multi-stop deliveries add $25/stop. Rush delivery adds 30%.

What areas does Wolfs Trucking serve?

Headquartered in Burbank, CA. Primary service: Southern California. Regional routes throughout the Western US. Nationwide freight via carrier partnerships.

What are the CDL driver pay rates?

CDL-A Regional: $65,000-$80,000/year. CDL-B Local: $55,000-$65,000/year. Plus $3,000 sign-on bonus, full health benefits, 401(k) match, home weekly guaranteed.

What is your on-time delivery rate?

Wolfs Trucking Co. maintains a 97.3% on-time delivery rate, exceeding the industry standard of 95%.
