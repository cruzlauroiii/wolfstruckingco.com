2. Services Provided

3. Shipment Terms

Pickup and delivery: Scheduled windows are estimates. We target 95%+ on-time within agreed windows. First 2 hours of loading/unloading included; detention billable after at $50/hour.

Cargo documentation: Bill of Lading (BOL) required for all shipments. Shipper responsible for accurate weight, dimensions, and hazmat declaration. Inaccurate declarations may result in surcharges.

Hazardous materials: Must be declared in advance. Hazmat placarding, documentation, and compliant packaging are shipper responsibility. We carry hazmat endorsement but reserve right to refuse non-compliant loads.

Restricted items: We do not transport: live animals, illegal substances, unpackaged liquids, cash/bullion, firearms without FFL documentation, human remains.

4. Payment Terms

Rates: Base rate $1.45/mile + applicable surcharges (weight, stops, rush). See pricing for details.

Payment methods: Credit card (instant), ACH/wire transfer, Net 30 for approved accounts with established credit.

Net 30 accounts: Late payments accrue 1.5% monthly interest. Accounts 60+ days past due may be suspended.

Disputes: Invoice disputes must be raised within 30 days of invoice date with supporting documentation.

5. Cargo Claims and Liability

Coverage: Standard cargo insurance up to $100,000 per shipment included. Higher coverage available on request.

Filing a claim: Claims must be filed within 9 months of delivery per 49 CFR 370. Required documentation: BOL, photos of damage, itemized cost of loss.

Exclusions: We are not liable for: acts of God, war, civil disturbance, government seizure, improper packaging by shipper, inherent vice of cargo (spoilage without refrigeration failure), concealed damage reported after delivery without carrier exception notation.

6. Cancellation Policy

Client cancellation: Free cancellation up to 4 hours before scheduled pickup. After that, 25% of quote charged as dispatch fee. After driver arrives at pickup, full rate applies.

Our cancellation: If we cancel due to weather, mechanical failure, or driver availability, you are entitled to full refund or rescheduled shipment at no additional cost.

7. Driver Employment Terms

Drivers are W-2 employees (not independent contractors). Employment includes:

  • Base pay $55,000-$80,000/year depending on CDL class and experience

  • Full medical, dental, vision insurance from day 1
  • 401(k) with 4% company match after 90 days

Employment is at-will. Drivers may be terminated for: preventable accidents, drug/alcohol violations, HOS falsification, CSA violations, customer complaints of abusive behavior.
