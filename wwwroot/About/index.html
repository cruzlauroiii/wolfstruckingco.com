Our Story

Wolfs Trucking Co. started with one truck, one driver, and one promise: move freight the way you would if it were your own. Founder Kei Chee drove that first load himself — a pallet of auto parts from Burbank to Commerce — and then dispatched it, invoiced it, and collected the check. That hands-on mindset hasn't changed.

Today we operate a 52-truck fleet of late-model Freightliner Cascadias and Peterbilt 579s out of our Burbank, CA headquarters. Our drivers are W-2 employees — not contractors — with full health benefits, 401(k) match, and home-weekly schedules. We believe the best way to take care of our customers is to take care of our drivers first.

We serve shippers ranging from regional manufacturers and retail distributors to ocean carriers and 3PL partners. Our lanes cover Southern California, Nevada, Arizona, and the Southeastern US with carrier partnerships for full nationwide coverage. Every load — whether $165 or $5,000 — gets the same level of attention.

What makes us different? We own our trucks. We employ our drivers. We answer our own phones. No broker layers, no hidden fees, no "check back tomorrow." Just $1.45/mile, instant quotes, and 97.3% on-time delivery.

Our Journey

From one truck to a full fleet in seven years.

2018

Founded in Burbank, CA

Kei Chee and one Freightliner Cascadia start hauling regional loads in Southern California.

2019

First Warehouse Opens

Moved into 40,000 sq ft cross-dock facility on Glenoaks Blvd. Added 3PL services.

2020

Essential Services Designation

Kept hauling through the pandemic. Medical supply and grocery freight priority. Fleet grew to 12 trucks.

2021

Port of LA Drayage Certified

TWIC fleet deployment. Became direct drayage carrier for Port of LA/Long Beach.

2022

SmartWay Excellence Partner

EPA SmartWay certification for fuel efficiency and emissions. Fleet upgraded to 30 trucks.

2023

CTA Safety Award

California Trucking Association Safety Award. 0 preventable accidents. Fleet at 45 trucks.

2024

52-Truck Fleet + Nationwide

Reached 52 trucks, 47 drivers, 180K sq ft warehouse. Partner network for nationwide coverage.

2026

Digital-First Platform

Launched instant-quote client portal and AI-driven interview process. Still a family business at heart.
