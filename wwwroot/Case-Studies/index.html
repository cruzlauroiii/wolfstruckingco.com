The Problem

Blesse Mfg was shipping 8-12 palletized loads per month on predictable routes from their Charlotte facility to customer DCs in Greenville, Gastonia, and Asheville. Each load went through a freight broker quoting $335-$380.

After a 2025 freight audit, Blesse discovered the actual carrier rate was $285 — they were paying 18-25% broker markup on every load, every month.

The Solution

Blesse contacted Wolfs Trucking directly to establish a dedicated-lane relationship. We set up:

  • 3 dedicated lanes covering their top destinations
  • Flat-rate pricing at $285 base rate with transparent accessorials
  • Scheduled pickup windows (Tue/Thu mornings) optimized for their production schedule
  • Direct dispatch access via our Client Portal for last-minute adjustments

The Results (After 18 Months)

  • $12,000 annual savings on freight spend
  • 99% on-time across 142 shipments (up from 91% with broker)
  • 2-hour average response time for urgent requests (vs. 6+ hours with broker)
  • Zero damaged loads — direct carrier accountability
Wolfs Trucking has been our dedicated carrier for 18 months now. On-time every time. The client portal with instant quotes has cut our logistics planning time in half. Still answers their own phones too, which is rare these days. Noah Blesse, Founder · Blesse Manufacturing Co.

The Problem

Acme Corp imports approximately 240 containers per year through the Port of LA, primarily via APM Terminals and Fenix Marine. Their previous drayage partner's average gate-out to delivery time was 5.8 hours, and they were incurring occasional demurrage when containers sat past last-free-day.

At $200/day demurrage × 3-4 incidents per year, plus slow turnaround blocking their warehouse receiving schedule, the indirect costs were significant.

The Solution

Wolfs Trucking assigned Acme a dedicated drayage coordinator and provided these capabilities:

  • Proactive appointment booking — Pier Pass scheduled 48 hours ahead of LFD
  • TWIC-certified drivers with terminal-specific familiarity (APM, Fenix, TraPac)
  • Chassis provided — no pool fees, no separate chassis charges
  • Real-time container tracking from gate-out through delivery
  • Priority dispatch for containers within 24 hours of LFD

The Results (Over 12 Months)

  • Average turnaround: 4.5 hours (down from 5.8 hrs — 22% improvement)
  • Zero demurrage incidents across all 240 containers
  • $48,000 total cost reduction (lower rates + no demurrage + faster warehouse throughput)
  • Dedicated coordinator eliminated 6+ hours/week of broker back-and-forth
We compared Wolfs against 3 brokers for our monthly drayage runs from the Port of LA. Wolfs came in 22% cheaper with better on-time performance. Easy decision. The real-time tracking map is a nice bonus for our warehouse team. Sarah Patel, Logistics Manager · Acme Corp

The Path

Wolfs Trucking has an internal program where warehouse associates who meet performance criteria after 6 months get company-sponsored CDL training. Lauro completed:

  • Month 1-6: Warehouse performance — zero safety incidents, perfect attendance
