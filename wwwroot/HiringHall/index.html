Applicant review

New applicants come in here after the Opus 4.7 intake. Review their uploaded documents, then approve with the right badges to hire them as a worker.

Badges

Credentials are high-level qualifications required to do a job. Tags are supporting attributes.

Roles

Types of work. Each role lists required badges — a worker can only be scheduled into a role they hold all of its badges for.

Workers

The backbone of the platform. Approved workers can be scheduled into roles they qualify for.

Customers

Organizations that schedule workers. Each can have multiple users, locations, and roles.

Scheduling

Acting as a customer — create a booking for a role. Auto-reassignment offers the shift down the pool until N workers accept.

Time sheets

Derived from completed schedules. Accept a completed timesheet to mark it ready for payroll, then export CSV.

Accounts

Every sign-in account (applicant, staff, employer, driver, investor) that the SignUp page has created. Each record is stored in R2 with its SHA-256 password hash.

Audit trail

Every /api/charge, /api/upload, approve, reject, and dispatcher call. Pulled live from the R2 audit collection.
