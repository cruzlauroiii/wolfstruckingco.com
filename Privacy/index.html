1. Information We Collect

Wolfs Trucking Co. ("we", "our", "us") collects information necessary to provide freight shipping and employment services:

  • Account information: Name, email, phone, company, billing address
  • Shipment data: Pickup/delivery addresses, cargo details, weight, scheduling, payment method
  • Driver application data: CDL credentials, employment history, MVR, DOT medical certification
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, device type, browser, IP address
  • Location data: GPS position of our trucks during active deliveries (not user location)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Fulfill freight shipments and coordinate deliveries
  • Process payments and issue invoices
  • Evaluate driver applications and manage employment
  • Send shipment tracking notifications and service updates
  • Improve our service through analytics
  • Comply with DOT, FMCSA, and state regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our terms

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Drivers: Shipment details needed for delivery (pickup, delivery, cargo, instructions)
  • Payment processors: Stripe for secure card processing. We never store card numbers.
  • Authentication providers: Google, GitHub, Microsoft, Okta (SSO)
  • Infrastructure: Cloudflare for hosting and R2 storage
  • Legal authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Cloudflare R2 with encryption at rest and in transit. Card payment details are tokenized via Stripe and never stored on our systems. We use HTTPS for all data transfer. Access to systems requires SSO authentication.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication (session tokens in localStorage) and analytics cookies to understand site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser, but authentication will not work without them.

6. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

California residents (CCPA) and EU residents (GDPR) have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request a copy of your data (portability)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Opt out of data processing
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights

To exercise your rights, email privacy@wolfstruckingco.com or call (818) 624-0454.

7. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Shipment records: 7 years (tax and regulatory requirement)
  • Driver files: Duration of employment + 3 years (DOT requirement)
  • Interview transcripts: 1 year after completion
  • Analytics data: 14 months anonymized
  • Payment records: 7 years (tax requirement)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us for immediate deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Material changes will be communicated via email to account holders and posted here with an updated "Last updated" date at least 30 days before taking effect.
