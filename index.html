Requirements

What you need to join the Wolfs Trucking team.

✓

Valid CDL License

Class A or Class B Commercial Driver's License with all required endorsements for your position.

✓

Clean MVR

No more than 2 moving violations in the last 3 years. No DUI/DWI convictions.

✓

2+ Years Experience

Minimum 2 years verifiable Class A or B driving experience in the last 5 years.

✓

DOT Physical

Current DOT medical card or ability to obtain one. Valid for at least 6 months.

✓

Drug Screen

Must pass pre-employment drug screen and participate in random testing program per DOT regulations.

✓

Background Check

Clean criminal background. Must be authorized to work in the United States.
