Pricing FAQ

How is distance calculated? ▾
We use real driving distance via OSRM routing — not straight-line miles. Includes actual road network, highway preferences, and avoids restricted zones.
Do you charge for empty miles or deadhead? ▾
No. Our $1.45/mile rate covers the loaded miles only. We absorb deadhead costs as part of operating 52 trucks with efficient dispatch.
What about loading/unloading time (detention)? ▾
First 2 hours included at pickup and delivery. After that, $50/hour detention. Most shippers finish in under 1 hour with appointment scheduling.
Do you offer volume discounts? ▾
Yes. 10% discount on 10+ loads/month, 15% on 50+ loads/month. Contact (818) 624-0454 to set up a dedicated account.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
Credit card (instant), ACH/wire transfer, and Net 30 terms for approved accounts with established credit.
Is there insurance included? ▾
Standard cargo insurance up to $100K included. Higher coverage available on request for high-value shipments (electronics, pharma, automotive).
